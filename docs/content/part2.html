Wires, Cables, Wifi

The Internet uses a collection of different methods to deliver data between its endpoints

These methods are the following:

  • Electricity

    • Used in copper wires for its low price, but presents data loss over long distances

  • Light

    • Used in fiber-optic cables due to its ability to deliver data across with high speed, but is expensive and hard to work with

  • Radio

    • Used in WiFi in order to wirelessly connect over short distances

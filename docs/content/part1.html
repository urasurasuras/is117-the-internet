What is the Internet?

"The Internet" was created in the 1970s by Vint Cerf and Bob Kahn first know as the ARPANET (Advanced Research Projects Agency Network
The idea was to make a information network topology that would survive a nuclear attack.
This made the Internet decentralized and enabled it to theoretically be controlled by everyone who uses it.
